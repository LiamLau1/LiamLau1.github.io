Hey! I'm Liam.

Welcome to my homepage!

I am a physics graudate student at Rutgers University, jointly advised by Piers Coleman and Ananda Roy. I am interested in the interface between condensed matter and quantum information physics. I fancy myself as a theorist, but I find a lot of joy in talking to people about experiments.
